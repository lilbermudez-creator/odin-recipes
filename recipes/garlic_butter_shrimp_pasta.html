Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

Index Page

About

Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta is a light but flavorful dish that combines tender shrimp sauteed in garlic and butter with a splash of fresh lemon juice for brightness. The shrimp and sauce are tossed with pasta-often spaghetti or linguine-making it a quick, refreshing meal that's rich without being heavy.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 lb large shrimp
  • 3tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 lemon
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2tbsp fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red Pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, melt 2 tbsp butter over medium heat. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp, season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until pink and opaque. Remove shrimp, set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add remaining butter, lemon juice, and lemon zest. Stir well.
  5. Add pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat. If dry, add a splash of reserved pasta water.
  6. Stir in Parmesan and parsley. Add shrimp back in and toss everything together.
  7. Serve warm with extra Parmesan and lemon wedges on the side.
